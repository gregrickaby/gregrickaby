👋 Hello there! I'm an Alabama-based [software engineer](/resume/), [self-taught photographer](https://www.flickr.com/photos/gregrickaby/), and [published author](https://www.amazon.com/author/gregrickaby). I've been building for the web since 1997, long enough to have worked through pretty much every major shift the industry has seen.

WordPress became my primary focus in 2006. Over nearly two decades I've worked across the full stack of what the platform offers: theme and plugin development, custom functionality, and performance optimization for a wide range of clients and use cases.

In 2019 I expanded into React and Next.js, driven by projects that called for more dynamic, app-like experiences. Both are now a regular part of how I work.

More recently I've moved into AI integration, focusing on prompt engineering and agentic workflows building systems where models plan, make decisions, and hand off tasks with minimal human intervention.

25 years in, I bring a mix of deep platform expertise and a practical understanding of when and how to reach for newer tools.

View [my resume](/resume/) to learn more.

## Photographer

In late 2019, I purchased a Lumix GX85 mirrorless camera and dedicated several months to taking courses, reading, practicing, and receiving feedback from my peers. I started out as a modest hobbyist but have since become a passionate enthusiast who appreciates various forms of photography, such as landscape, events, wildlife, street, and astrophotography.

Check out [my portfolio](https://www.flickr.com/photos/gregrickaby/).
